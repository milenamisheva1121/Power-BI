# Power BI Dashboards Collection

A showcase of high-impact Power BI reports focusing on a structured, KPI-driven approach to business analytics. These dashboards demonstrate the integration of business intelligence, robust data modeling, advanced DAX logic, and user-centered design.

> **Note:** These reports utilize **dummy data** for demonstration purposes. Only visual previews (images) are included; the `.pbix` source files are not provided in this repository.

---

## üìä Report 1: Product Performance & Returns Analysis
A business-oriented report designed to support commercial and operational decision-making by tracking sales performance and return behaviors.

### Business Objectives
* **Performance Tracking:** Identify best-performing products and categories.
* **Quality Control:** Detect products with high return rates to monitor product quality.
* **Customer Insights:** Explore market trends and patterns across customer segments and regions.

### Technical Implementation
* **Data Prep:** Cleaning and transformation via **Power Query**.
* **Modeling:** Structured Star Schema (Fact and Dimension tables).
* **Geographical Analysis:** Filled maps and bubble maps for regional performance.
* **UX/UI:** Bookmark-driven navigation and drill-through pages.

---

## üìà Report 2: Collections, Risk & Payments Performance
A highly advanced, interactive dashboard focused on operational KPIs, risk exposure, and anomaly detection.

### Analytical Scope
* **Monthly KPIs:** Collection Balance (Fraud vs. Non-Fraud) and Collection Rates.
* **Trend Analysis:** Payment performance and negative merchant tracking (Top 10).
* **Efficiency Evaluation:** Analysis of collection agency performance.

### Technical Implementation
* **Advanced DAX:** Complex time intelligence, ranking logic, and measures driven by **Parameter Tables**.
* **Interactive Navigation:** Guided analysis flows using bookmarks and toggle-able perspectives.
* **Dynamic Perspectives:** Users can switch metrics and views dynamically.

---

## üí° Key DAX Highlights
Across both reports, the following logic patterns were implemented:
* **Time Intelligence:** YoY/MoM trends and comparisons.
* **Ranking Logic:** Top N analysis for products and merchants.
* **Dynamic Measures:** Utilizing parameter tables to change metrics on the fly.
